Meeting Script (20 minutes)

1) 0-3 min: Opening — Why this matters to constituents (two brief examples).
2) 3-8 min: Problem statement — what predictive systems can do and current legal gap.
3) 8-12 min: The ask — explain human-review and notice requirements; show draft language.
4) 12-16 min: Implementation — low-cost compliance steps for agencies; recordkeeping.
5) 16-20 min: Next steps — ask for staff contact, committee referral suggestions, offer to provide fiscal note language.

Leave-behind: One-pager + 3 bullet talking points.
