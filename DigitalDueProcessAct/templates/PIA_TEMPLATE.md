Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) — {SHORT TITLE}

1. Project description
- Scope, systems, and data types involved.

2. Purpose and legal authority
- Why the system is used and under what statutory authority.

3. Data categories and sources
- Personal, sensitive, de-identified, inferred data.

4. Access and sharing
- Who can access outputs and internal controls.

5. Risks and mitigations
- Bias, accuracy, false positives/negatives, data retention.

6. Notice and redress
- How affected individuals will be informed and can seek review.

7. Recordkeeping and auditability
- Logs, versioning, and retention periods.

8. Recommendation and decision
- Pilot suggested? Approval, conditions, and implementation timeline.
