Press announcement draft — Minnesota Digital Due Process Act packet

Headline: New neutral packet provides model policy to preserve due process in algorithmic government decisions

Lead paragraph: Today the Bill of Digital Rights project released a neutral, committee-ready packet on the Minnesota Digital Due Process Act. The packet includes a one-page summary, legislative brief, draft statutory language, and implementation checklist for agency pilots.

Call to action: The packet is publicly available for download and use by legislators, staff, civic groups, and volunteers. Visit [repo link] to download and to learn how to request a briefing.
