Meeting script — 10 minute staff briefing

1. Quick intro (1 minute)
- Thank you for your time. I'm {Name} from {Organization}. We prepared a short packet on {SHORT TITLE}.

2. One-page summary (2 minutes)
- State the problem in one sentence, the proposed solution in one sentence, and the operational safeguards (human review, notice, recordkeeping).

3. Why this matters (3 minutes)
- Emphasize constitutional protections, minimal implementation burden, and pilot-friendly approach. Offer implementation checklist and training plan.

4. Ask (2 minutes)
- Offer to provide a printed packet, follow up with technical staff, or provide a short presentation to the committee.

5. Close (2 minutes)
- Provide contact info, ask permission to send the packet, and thank them for time.

Notes:
- Keep it non-partisan and procedural.
- Offer alternatives (agency pilot, public comment period) instead of only statutory change.
