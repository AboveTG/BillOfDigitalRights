Printing cost estimates (US, June 2026)

Estimate methodology: small-batch printing at local copy shops (FedEx Office, Staples, independent print shops). Prices vary by region.

Per-packet estimate (20 pages, double-sided, black & white):
- Per-page B&W duplex: $0.03–$0.08
- Pages: 20 => $0.60–$1.60
- Staple/finish: $0.10–$0.50
- Per-packet estimate: ~$0.70–$2.10

One-pager only (single sheet, color front): $0.50–$1.25 each depending on volume.

Savings tips:
- Print in batches of 25–100 to reduce per-unit costs.
- Use local community print co-ops or library services for lower rates.
- Distribute electronically (PDF) whenever possible to eliminate costs entirely.

Instructions for ordering at a local shop:
1. Export PDFs of the final packet (one file with correct page order).
2. Specify duplex (double-sided), black & white.
3. Request stapling in the upper-left corner, unless binding is preferred.
4. Ask for a proof copy before printing the full batch.
