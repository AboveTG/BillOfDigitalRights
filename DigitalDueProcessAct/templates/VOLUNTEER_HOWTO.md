Volunteer outreach how-to — {SHORT TITLE}

Goal: distribute packets, collect signatures/endorsements, and request meetings without paid staff.

Steps:
1. Download the neutral packet PDF and local print instructions.
2. Identify local legislators and their staff email addresses.
3. Send the templated outreach email and follow up within one week.
4. Offer a printed packet or an in-person 10-minute briefing using the meeting script.
5. Track responses in a simple spreadsheet: name, office, date contacted, response, next step.
6. Ask for a staff contact for technical follow-up and a copy of any committee packet filing procedures.

Tips:
- Keep messages neutral and procedural to attract bipartisan attention.
- Recruit local law students or volunteers to handle printing and distribution.
- Use community spaces (libraries, civic groups) to host small briefings.
